Biography

Zoltán Szántó is a lecturer at Sapientia Hungarian University of Transylvania. He is with the Department of Electrical Engineering, and he teaches Software Engineering. His research interests include networked robotics, cyber-security and swarm robotics. He is a member of the TARC (https://ms.sapientia.ro/en/research-groups/department-of-electrical-engineering/computer-science/tarc) research group which focuses on the Control of teleoperated and autonomous robots. He can usually be found in the Networked and Autonomous Robot Control Systems laboratory, NACS (https://erris.gov.ro/nacs).
